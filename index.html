Interactive Lesson Plan

Grade 7: Introduction to Desktop Publishing (Canva)

Lesson Objectives

At the end of this 40-minute lesson, students will be able to:

  • ✔ Define what desktop publishing is and give examples of its use.
  • ✔ Identify the basic interface elements of Canva for Education.
  • ✔ Explain the purpose and function of templates in graphic design.
  • ✔ Participate in a guided activity to explore templates and design ideas.

Materials Required

LCD Projector
Whiteboard
Marker
Paper for Students

Lesson Procedure (40 Minutes)

Click on each step below to view the detailed activities and instructions for that part of the lesson.

I. Introduction

5 Mins

Hook (2 mins)

Begin by asking students to look at posters, flyers, or invitations. Ask: "How do you think these things are made? What tools do people use to combine text, images, and colors to create them?"

Topic Introduction (3 mins)

Introduce the concept of Desktop Publishing (DTP) as the process of using a computer and software to create visual documents. Explain that today, they'll learn about a powerful tool called Canva for Education.

II. Exploring the Canva Interface

10 Mins

Teacher's Role & Activity

Project the Canva homepage. As you navigate, point to and explain the main parts of the interface:

  • Homepage: Show recent designs, folders, and main navigation.
  • Search Bar: Explain its power for finding design types or ideas.
  • Left-Hand Panel: Point out "Templates," "Elements," "Text," and "Uploads" as their "toolboxes."
  • Filipino Context: Demonstrate a search for a relevant Filipino theme (e.g., "Buwan ng Wika") to make it relatable.

III. Exploring Templates

15 Mins

Teacher's Role & Activity

Continue the live demonstration, explaining that templates are pre-made, customizable designs that save time and effort.

  1. Search for various poster templates related to school events or Filipino festivals.
  2. Open a few different templates to show the class what they look like.
  3. Guided Inquiry: Ask students which templates they like and why. Ask them to identify the different elements (text, images, background).

IV. Bonus: Introduction to AI in Design

5 Mins

Teacher's Role & Activity

Briefly introduce the concept of AI in Design. Explain that some tools in Canva use artificial intelligence. Mention features like "Magic Edit" (which can remove or replace objects) to illustrate how AI makes design easier and faster. Note that this is a preview for future hands-on lessons.

V. Conclusion & Assessment

5 Mins

Review (2 mins)

Do a quick recap. Ask students to name one thing they learned about the Canva interface and one thing they learned about templates.

Assessment Activity (3 mins)

Distribute blank paper. Ask students to imagine creating a poster for a school event and to:

  1. Write down the event title.
  2. Sketch out their poster idea (text/image placement).
  3. Write down which type of Canva template they would use to start.

Collect the papers as a simple assessment of their understanding.
